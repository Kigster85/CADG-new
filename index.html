Who are we?


All of us have come from some form of I.T. background and have a passion for gaming, all things tech, space, and generally having a good time amongst good company.


Having been part of many clans and communities over the years, it was decided it would be a fun little endevour to start up our own community. Ctrl Alt Del Gaming was born.


Bringing together some great minds. Our plan was put into place, and this is just the beginning. With lots more planned for the future and what we have on offer. We don't believe in pay to win, we like to be fair to all so you won't find any pay to win scenario on any of our servers.


Things in mind for the future include:- New servers | twitch interactive game mod's | our own in-house designed bots for twitch & discord | & much much more!

Community


Community is something very important to us, working together, gaming together, and having fun whilst we do it. Being part of the community has many benefits, unlock unique members only features on our server. These are small quality of life changes which don't give any in game advantages.
Build long lasting friendships with others who enjoy the games you do.Work together with other community members on coding problems or mods which all members will have special access to use.
